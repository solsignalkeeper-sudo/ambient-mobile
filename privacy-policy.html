Privacy Policy – Ambient Encouragement

Last updated: January 16, 2026

Ambient Encouragement respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • The app does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Any custom phrases you enter are stored locally on your device.
  • Text used to generate audio is sent to a third-party text-to-speech service (ElevenLabs) solely to produce spoken audio.

How Information Is Used

  • To generate spoken encouragement and ambient audio.
  • To operate and maintain the core functionality of the app.

Data Sharing

  • Audio text is sent to ElevenLabs for speech generation.
  • No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Data Storage

  • The app does not require user accounts.
  • No personal data is stored on our servers.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: ambient.encouragement.app@gmail.com
